Prediction Engine — Pattern & Signal

Enable as many base patterns as you like — each one runs completely independently with its own filter toggles below it. When more than one enabled pattern fires on the same candle, priority order (ATR Reversal → Engulfing → Hammer/SS → Exhaustion) picks the winner for that candle.

ATR/ATR SMA above are global — they feed the F2/F5 filters (and the ATR Reversal threshold) no matter which patterns below are enabled.

Early Entry: checks the still-forming candle's live (not-yet-closed) body against the pattern in the last few seconds before it closes. If it already matches, Live Trades can enter on it immediately instead of waiting for the close. Trades already entered off an early signal are never cancelled if the true close later disagrees — only a log line records the mismatch.

Live Trades — Trading Engine

Every trade's stake is sized live by Money Management's settings, not a flat amount here — see the Money Management page. A candidate whose window has already opened for more than the Entry Deadline below with no entry yet is skipped instead of entered late — see Trade History's "Skipped (Late)" rows. Tune the deadline to match your entry mode: short (e.g. 10s) for Immediate Entry, longer (e.g. 130s) to give the order-book recovery-entry mode room to find its setup.

Immediate Entry only buys from this many seconds before the candle opens to this many seconds after — outside that window it holds off even if the profit factor floor is met (the candidate keeps observing until Entry Deadline above eventually skips it).
While any candidate is still observing (no order placed yet) and within Fast-Poll Lead Time of its candle's open — on either side, up to whichever entry cutoff currently applies (Immediate Entry Window, or Entry Deadline otherwise) — the whole engine ticks at Fast-Poll Tick Interval instead of Refresh Interval, to catch the exact right price. Falls back to Refresh Interval as soon as an order is placed or that window closes.

Backup & Restore

Export bundles all settings and the complete trade database into one file. Import restores both from a previously exported file, replacing everything currently running — export right after importing and you'll get the exact same file back, byte for byte.

Danger Zone

Permanently deletes every candidate, trade, and snapshot (Live Trades, Trade Details, Trade History, and Money Management all go back to a clean slate — Money Management's balance and loss basket reset to your starting balance since they're computed from this same history). Currently open trades are abandoned, not settled. This cannot be undone.
